At SnapIT Analytics, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website analytics service.

Key Points:
  • We are GDPR compliant
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • You control your analytics data
  • We use industry-standard encryption

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account via Google OAuth, we collect:

  • Name: Your full name from your Google account
  • Email Address: Your email address for account identification and communication
  • Profile Picture: Your Google profile photo
  • Google ID: A unique identifier from Google

1.2 Analytics Data (Your Website's Visitors)

When you implement our tracking code on your websites, we collect analytics data about your visitors:

  • Page Views: URLs visited, page titles, timestamps
  • User Behavior: Click events, scroll depth, time on page
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, screen resolution, operating system
  • Location Data: Country and city (derived from IP address, IP not stored)
  • Referrer Information: Where visitors came from
  • Custom Events: Events you choose to track

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about your use of our Service:

  • Login timestamps and frequency
  • Features accessed within the dashboard
  • API requests made
  • Error logs and debugging information

1.4 Payment Information

For paid subscriptions:

  • Processed by Stripe: We use Stripe for payment processing and do NOT store your credit card information
  • What we store: Stripe customer ID, subscription status, billing history

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide the Service

  • Process and display your website analytics
  • Generate reports and visualizations
  • Provide API access to your data
  • Send tracking codes and configuration instructions

2.2 To Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug errors and optimize performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send account-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important service updates (security, terms changes)
  • Send optional marketing communications (with your consent)

2.4 For Billing and Administration

  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information or analytics data to third parties.

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google OAuth Authentication Name, email, profile picture
Stripe Payment Processing Email, billing information
AWS Infrastructure & Hosting All service data (encrypted)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Requests from law enforcement or government agencies
  • Protection of our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Prevention of fraud or security threats

3.3 Business Transfers

If SnapIT Software is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring and alerts
  • Secure Infrastructure: AWS security best practices

4.2 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Analytics Data

  • Free Plan: 30 days
  • Basic Plan: 90 days
  • Pro Plan: 6 months
  • Business Plan: 1 year
  • Enterprise/Agency: 2 years or custom

5.2 Account Information

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • 30-day grace period (data recoverable)
  • Permanent deletion after 30 days
  • Billing records retained for 7 years (legal requirement)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings.

6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data by closing your account.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your analytics data in JSON or CSV format through the dashboard or API.

6.5 Right to Object

You can object to processing of your personal data for marketing purposes.

6.6 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request restriction of processing under certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@snapitsoftware.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Our Website Cookies

SnapIT Analytics uses cookies on our own website for:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in
  • Preferences: To remember your settings
  • Analytics: To improve our website (we use our own analytics!)

7.2 Your Website Tracking

The tracking code you install on your websites:

  • Does NOT use third-party cookies
  • Uses localStorage for session tracking
  • Is GDPR compliant by default
  • Can be configured to respect "Do Not Track" signals

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • EU-US Data Privacy Framework participation (pending)

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information

  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
